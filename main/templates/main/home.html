About

CodeFlower is a website that can help anyone learn the fundamental basics of computer programming. Whether you are hoping to pick up programming as a hobby, interested in obtaining a Computer Science degree, or looking for a new hobby, CodeFlower will help you explore the wonderful world of programming!

How it works

CodeFlower offers programming topics selected to give you a basic idea of how computer programming works. Once you select a topic you are interested in, you may work through lessons and test your code in the browser so you can implement what you have learned. Once you are done with a lesson, you will have the option to take a quiz to solidify your understanding and move to the next lesson/topic.
